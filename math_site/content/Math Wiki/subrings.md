## Definition
A **subring** of a [ring]({{< ref rings.md >}}) $(R, +, \cdot)$ is a subset $S \subset R$ such that $(S, +, \cdot)$ is also a ring. 

## Properties
A set $S$ is a subring of a ring $R$ if and only if 

- $S \neq \emptyset$;
- $rs \in S$ for all $r,s \in S$;
- $r - s \in S$ for all $r,s \in S$.
