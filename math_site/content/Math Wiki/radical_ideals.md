## Definition
An [ideal]({{< ref ideals.md >}}) $I$ of a [ring]({{< ref rings.md >}}) $R$ is called a **radical ideal** if $a^m \in I$ for some integer $m \geq 1$ implies that $a \in I$. 

## Properties
All [maximal ideals]({{< ref maximal_ideals.md >}}) are radical ideals.
