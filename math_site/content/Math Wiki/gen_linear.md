## Definition
The **general linear group**, denoted $GL_n(\F)$, is the [group]({{< ref groups.md >}}) of $n$-by-$n$ [invertible]({{< ref invertibility.md >}}) [matrices]({{< ref matrices.md >}}) over the [field]({{< ref fields.md >}}) $\F$.
